Mojarra frita recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 mojarra fish, gutted, 0.8 pounds
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • lime peels
  • black pepper
  • 1 liter vegetable oil
  • flour

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a skillet. Choose a deep skillet to dip the fish.
  2. Clean mojarra and dry it. Make shallow cuts, diagonally. Season with salt and pepper. Dip in flour.
  3. Add garlic and lime peel to skillet with oil. Once the oil is burning hot, add mojarra. Fry for about 15 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Place fried mojarra in a sieve and pat-dry with paper towels to remove excess oil.
